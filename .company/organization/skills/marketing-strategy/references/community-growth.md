## Growth Tactics

### 1. Content Consistency

**Strategy:**
- 1-2 tweets/day (午後優先)
- 個人的ストーリー30%
- 技術的価値提供40%

**Expected:**
- +50 followers/week (organic)
- +200 followers/month

### 2. Engagement Amplification

**Reply Strategy:**
- **50% reply rate** (現状30%)
- 優先順位:
  1. Questions（質問には必ず返信）
  2. Feedback（ポジティブ・ネガティブ両方）
  3. High-value followers（DeFi builders）

**RT Strategy:**
- RT with commentary（単純RTの2x engagement）
- 1日3-5 RTs
- Focus: DeFi news, Solana updates, User content

**Like Strategy:**
- 1日20-30 likes
- Followers' good content
- Relevant DeFi discussions

**Expected:**
- +30% engagement rate
- +100 followers/month

### 3. AMA Series

**Frequency:**
- 月2回（第1・第3金曜 15:00 JST）

**Format:**
- X Space (live audio)
- 30-45分
- Q&A focus

**Topics:**
- Week 1: Product updates
- Week 3: DeFi trends + Community Q&A

**Expected:**
- +20 followers/AMA
- +40 followers/month

### 4. Collaborations

**Target Collaborators:**
- Solana DeFi builders
- Superteam JP members
- Crypto educators

**Format:**
- Joint X Space
- Guest threads
- Podcast appearances

**Frequency:**
- 月1-2回

**Expected:**
- +50 followers/collaboration
- +100 followers/month

### 5. Community Highlights

**Strategy:**
- Weekly user spotlight
- Share community wins
- Feature feedback

**Example:**
"@user123 just made their first Perp trade on Axis Pizza. Welcome to DeFi! 🎉"

**Expected:**
- Community loyalty增加
- Organic shares
- +50 followers/month

## Community Culture

### Values

1. **Build in Public**
   - Share wins + failures
   - Transparent roadmap
   - Open feedback

2. **Support First**
   - Helpful community
   - No toxic behavior
   - Encourage questions

3. **User-Centric**
   - Listen to feedback
   - Fast iteration
   - User-driven features

### Guidelines

**Encouraged:**
- Questions（何でも聞いてOK）
- Feedback（ポジティブ・ネガティブ両方）
- Sharing experiences
- Helping others

**Discouraged:**
- FUD spreading
- Spam
- Self-promotion without value
- Toxic behavior

## Measurement & Optimization

### Weekly Review

**Check:**

2. Engagement rate trends
3. Top engaging content

- Content mix
- Engagement tactics
- AMA topics

### Monthly Review

**Check:**
1. Goal progress（Followers, Engagement, Users）
2. Best/worst performing tactics
3. Community health（sentiment, activity）
4. Competitor benchmarks

**Adjust:**
- Strategy pivots
- Resource allocation
- New tactics testing
