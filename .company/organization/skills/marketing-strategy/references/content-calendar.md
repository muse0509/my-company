# Content Calendar テンプレート

週次・月次コンテンツ計画フレームワーク。

## Weekly Template

### Monday
**テーマ:** Week Kickoff
- Weekend recap
- Week ahead preview
- 1 key goal

**Example:**
"Weekend spent shipping Drift integration. This week: Jupiter Perps + UI polish. Let's go! 🚀"

**投稿時間:** 15:00 JST

---

### Tuesday
**テーマ:** Technical Content
- DeFi protocol解説
- Solana tech update
- Code snippet/architecture

**Example:**
"How Drift Perps work: Imagine trading with 10x leverage but auto-risk management. Here's the flow..."

**投稿時間:** 15:00 JST

---

### Wednesday
**テーマ:** Axis Pizza Update
- Feature announcement
- User feedback response
- Behind-the-scenes

**Example:**
"127 users asked for mobile app. Building it now. Here's the mockup 👇"

**投稿時間:** 15:00 JST

---

### Thursday
**テーマ:** Community Highlight
- User story
- Community member spotlight
- Feedback appreciation

**Example:**
"Shoutout to @user123 for finding that UI bug. Fixed in 2 hours. This is why I build in public."

**投稿時間:** 15:00 JST

---

### Friday
**テーマ:** Week Wrap + AMA (if scheduled)
- Week accomplishments
- AMA announcement (bi-weekly)
- Weekend plans

**Example:**
"Week done: Drift integration live, 20 new users, 0 bugs. AMA tomorrow 15:00 - ask anything!"

**投稿時間:** 15:00 JST

---

### Saturday
**テーマ:** Personal Story
- Learning + Reflection
- Failure + Growth
- Behind-the-scenes life

**Example:**
"Failed my first VC pitch last week. Learned: thesis fit > product perfection. Rebuilding deck."

**投稿時間:** 14:00 JST（週末は少し早め）

---

### Sunday
**テーマ:** Inspiration + Rest
- Industry news share
- Motivational thought
- Week ahead teaser

**Example:**
"Jupiter hit $10B volume. Proof that Solana DeFi is just getting started. Axis Pizza next 👀"

**投稿時間:** 14:00 JST

## Content Batching

### Batch Creation (Weekly)

**Sunday Prep:**
1. X Post Writer (Haiku) に7日分下書き依頼
2. Review + Edit（30分）
3. Schedule in Buffer/Hootsuite

**Benefits:**
- Consistency維持
- 時間節約（集中作業）
- 戦略的mix確保

### Emergency Buffer

**5個常にストック:**
- Axis Pizza update x2
- DeFi news share x2
- Personal story x1

**使用タイミング:**
- 忙しくて投稿できない日
- 予定変更
- 緊急対応時

## Content Mix Ratios

**Daily (1-2 tweets):**
- 40%: Technical/Educational
- 30%: Axis Pizza updates
- 20%: Community/Personal
- 10%: Industry news share

**Weekly (7-14 tweets):**
- 3-4: Technical content
- 2-3: Axis Pizza
- 1-2: Personal stories
- 1-2: Community/AMA

## Review & Optimization

### Weekly Review (Every Sunday)

**Check:**
1. Which tweets performed best?
2. Optimal posting times confirmed?
3. Content mix aligned?
4. Engagement rate trends?

**Adjust:**
- 次週のトピック調整
- 投稿時間微調整
- 新フォーマット実験

### Monthly Review

**Metrics:**
- Follower growth
- Engagement rate
- Top 10 tweets analysis
- Best time/format/topic

**Strategy:**
- 次月focus決定
- Content mix調整
- 新施策テスト
