**Monday Delivery:**

**1. Market Overview**
- Solana DeFi TVL trend（week-over-week）

- Volume trends

**2. Competitive Landscape**
- Drift/Jupiter/Marginfi updates
- New entrants
- Feature launches

**3. User Behavior**
- Active addresses trend
- Transaction volume
- Gas fee trends

**4. Insights**
- Key takeaways（2-3 bullets）
- Opportunities for Axis Pizza
- Risks/Challenges

**Example:**
```
# Weekly Market Report - March 10, 2025

## Market Overview
- Solana DeFi TVL: $8.2B (+2.5% WoW)
- Perps Volume: $52B (+4% WoW)
- Active addresses: 1.2M (+5% WoW)

## Competitive Landscape
- Jupiter launched Perps v2 (better UX)
- Drift hit 100K users milestone
- New entrant: Phoenix (Orderbook DEX)

## User Behavior

- Avg trade size down 10% (retail influx)
- Gas fees stable (~$0.001/txn)

## Insights
- Mobile DeFi accelerating (opportunity for Axis Pizza mobile app)
- Retail users growing (validates UX simplification thesis)
- Jupiter Perps v2 = competitive threat (need to differentiate)
```

## Analysis Frameworks

### TAM/SAM/SOM

**TAM（Total Addressable Market）:**
- All crypto users globally: ~500M
- DeFi users: ~50M（10%）

**SAM（Serviceable Addressable Market）:**
- Solana DeFi users: ~1M（2%）

**SOM（Serviceable Obtainable Market）:**
- Axis Pizza target: 100K（10% of SAM）
- Current: 127（0.01% of SAM）
- Growth needed: 1000x

### Growth Curve Analysis

**S-Curve Pattern:**
1. **Innovators（0-2.5%）:** Early adopters
2. **Early Adopters（2.5-16%）:** Growth phase
3. **Early Majority（16-50%）:** Mass adoption
4. **Late Majority（50-84%）:** Maturity
5. **Laggards（84-100%）:** Decline

**Axis Pizza Position:**
- Current: Innovators phase（127 users）
- Target Q2: Early Adopters（1000 users）
- Target Q4: Early Majority（10K users）

### Porter's Five Forces

**1. Competitive Rivalry（High）:**
- Drift, Jupiter, Marginfi
- Low switching costs
- Differentiation crucial

**2. Threat of New Entrants（Medium）:**
- Low barriers（Solana permissionless）
- Network effects protect incumbents

**3. Bargaining Power of Suppliers（Low）:**
- Underlying protocols（Drift, Jupiter）公開

**4. Bargaining Power of Buyers（High）:**
- Users can easily switch
- UX = key retention factor

**5. Threat of Substitutes（Medium）:**
- CEXs（Binance, etc.）
- Other chains（Ethereum, Base）

**Axis Pizza Strategy:**
- Compete on UX（not liquidity）
- Build switching costs（portfolio, social）
- Target underserved segment（newbies）

## Insight Generation

### Pattern Recognition

**Example 1: Jupiter Growth**
- **Pattern:** Swap success → Perps launch
- **Insight:** Strong brand enables new products
- **Application:** Axis Pizza should build brand early

**Example 2: Drift Liquidity**
- **Pattern:** Liquidity first → User growth
- **Insight:** Liquidity attracts traders
- **Application:** Axis Pizza = UX first, partner for liquidity

**Example 3: Mobile DeFi**
- **Pattern:** Solana Mobile → Wallet growth
- **Insight:** Mobile = next frontier
- **Application:** Axis Pizza mobile app = priority

### Trend Extrapolation

**Method:**
1. Identify current trend（Mobile DeFi +15%/month）
2. Project forward（6 months = 2x users）
3. Assess Axis Pizza positioning

**Example:**
- Trend: Mobile DeFi users +15%/month
- Projection: 6 months = 1M → 2M mobile users
- Action: Axis Pizza mobile app launch Q2 = perfect timing
