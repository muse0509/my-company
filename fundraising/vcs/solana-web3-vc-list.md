### Warm Intro Targets:

- Leverage Solana Foundation relationships
- Twitter engagement first (tag in technical threads)

### Materials Needed Before Outreach:
- [ ] Pitch deck (10-12 slides)
- [ ] Product demo (live or video)
- [ ] Use of Proceeds ($500K ask)
- [ ] Token economics draft
- [ ] Technical whitepaper/litepaper
- [ ] Team bios with LinkedIn
- [ ] Traction metrics (if available)

### Email Template Structure:
1. **Subject:** Brief hook (60 chars max)
2. **Opening:** Personal connection or why this VC specifically
3. **Problem:** 1-2 sentences
4. **Solution:** 2-3 sentences
5. **Traction:** Metrics if available
6. **Ask:** Meeting request, timeline
7. **Deck:** Attached or link

### Follow-up Cadence:
- Initial email → Wait 5 business days
- Follow-up 1 → Wait 7 business days
- Follow-up 2 → Final attempt
- Move to next tier if no response
